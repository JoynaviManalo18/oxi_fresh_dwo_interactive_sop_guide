Before you start
  • Read Appointment Notes carefully — pets, special instructions, precalls
  • Verify customer name, address, and invoice number
  • Tap the blue Begin Job button to start the job timer
Tap Begin Job FIRST — always. This records your arrival time and starts your Sales Per Hour (SPH) calculation.

Walkthrough & Confirm Quote
3 tabs: Services · General Notes · Waivers
Services
General Notes
Waivers
This tab shows the original booking quote. Assess actual on-site conditions and adjust.
  • Review all booked services with the customer
  • If actual items are LESS than booked — use the ( - ) button to reduce quantity
  • If actual items are SAME as booked — no changes needed
  • If ADDITIONAL rooms or items found on-site — add them in Step 3
Do NOT add new upsells or services here. Step 2 = adjust quantities only. Step 3 = add new services.
  • Add overall job notes in the top box
  • Add per-service notes for stains, damage, or special requests
  • For cancellations — document condition, reason, and recommendation here
Good notes protect you and Oxi Fresh. Document any pre-existing issues so there is no dispute later.
No digital signature exists. Customer must type their name or initials in the Notes field of each waiver to confirm approval.
6 waiver types — select the right one
Invoice UpdatedEvery time invoice changes or upsell accepted
Declined Recommended Service(s)Customer says NO to any recommended service
Damage / Pre-Existing ConditionsDamage or stains found before cleaning
Unknown / Unapproved MaterialMaterial unknown or not standard
Replacement RecommendedTech recommends replacement, customer proceeds
Custom WaiverSpecial situations not covered above

3
Recommend Services Upsells here
Add additional rooms, items & advance treatments not in original booking
This is the correct tab for ALL upsells, additional rooms, and advance treatments found on-site.
  • Review Current Services summary with the customer
  • Recommend advance treatments — Pet Treatment, Odor Destroyer, Sanitizer, Protectant, Spotting Kit
  • Customer ACCEPTS — tap + Add Another Service, then go to Step 2 → Waivers Tab → add Invoice Updated waiver
  • Customer DECLINES — go to Step 2 → Waivers Tab → add Declined Recommended Service(s) waiver
  • Apply coupon if applicable — tap + Add Coupon
Mention protectant or sanitizer for customers with kids or pets — a quick recommendation increases job value!
4
Waivers — review screen
Confirm all waivers added in Step 2 are complete
Step 4 is a review only screen. Waivers are added in Step 2 → Waivers Tab using the Select a Waiver dropdown. If any are missing, go back to Step 2.
  • Confirm all required waivers are showing as completed
  • Confirm customer name or initials are in the Notes field of each waiver
  • If any waiver is missing — go BACK to Step 2 → Waivers Tab to add it
Bypass should NEVER be used as a shortcut. Always call Joy or Sean before bypassing any waiver.
5
Final Review
Last chance to verify everything before taking payment
  • Review all services and quantities with the customer
  • Verify all discounts are showing correctly
  • Add service notes in the Final Notes box (e.g. "CASH PAYMENT" if applicable)
  • Tap Upload Photos to take AFTER photos of each cleaned area
